Travel and Expense Supplement and Thresholds Standard - Orphoz Public

This supplement applies to Firm members assigned to COMPANY Public Germany.

Travel support is provided through the assigned travel team. Firm members should use the designated travel contact channels and follow local support hours when arranging or changing travel.

Hotel bills must be submitted correctly and addressed to:

- COMPANY 

Rail travel is the default mode of travel. Economy flights may be used when rail travel is not efficient for the itinerary.

Short-haul flights on certain routes are not reimbursable unless they are part of a long-haul connecting flight.

Firm members should use preferred hotels in major cities when available. If a preferred hotel is not available or is not suitable, the lowest suitable rate should be selected, up to EUR 150 unless another approved threshold applies.

Preferred hotel guidance may apply in cities including:

- Berlin
- Koln
- Nurnberg
- Munchen
- Frankfurt
- Dusseldorf
- Hamburg
- Stuttgart
- Wien

CST team dinners may be held occasionally for appropriate celebratory purposes. The maximum cost is EUR 100 per person, no more than once per month, charged to the client code, and aligned with the responsible partner.
